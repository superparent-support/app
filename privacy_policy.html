Privacy Policy for SuperParent

Last Updated: November 29, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to SuperParent ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and the privacy of your children. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

This app is designed to help parents teach fascinating facts to their children daily. We understand the importance of protecting children's privacy and comply with all applicable laws including the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We collect the following information when you use SuperParent:

  • Account Information: Email address and name when you register using Google Sign-In or email/password authentication
  • Profile Information: Optional profile details you choose to provide
  • Usage Data: Information about which facts you view, complete, or save for later
  • Device Information: Device tokens for push notifications
  • Progress Data: Your score, achievement levels, and completion history

2.2 Children's Information

Important: SuperParent does NOT collect any personal information from children. The app is designed for parents to use to teach their children. Only parents create accounts and use the app. We do not knowingly collect, store, or process any information from children under 13.

2.3 Automatically Collected Information

  • App usage analytics (via Firebase Analytics)
  • Crash reports and diagnostic data
  • Ad interaction data (via Google AdMob)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Service Delivery: To provide daily parenting facts and educational content
  • Personalization: To remember your preferences, notification schedules, and progress
  • Notifications: To send scheduled reminders and special announcements
  • Achievement Tracking: To track your progress and unlock achievements
  • App Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve our service
  • Communication: To respond to your inquiries and provide support

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share information with:

4.1 Service Providers

  • Firebase (Google): For authentication, database, storage, and analytics
  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements
  • Google Cloud Functions: For backend processing

4.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or to:

  • Comply with legal processes
  • Protect our rights and property
  • Prevent fraud or security issues
  • Protect user safety

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Secure authentication via Firebase Authentication
  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/SSL)
  • Firestore security rules to protect user data
  • Regular security updates and monitoring

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Advertising

SuperParent displays advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may use:

  • Device advertising ID
  • General location information
  • App usage data for ad personalization

You can opt-out of personalized ads through your device settings. We ensure all ads comply with family-friendly content policies.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Correction: Update or correct your information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt-Out: Disable push notifications in app settings
  • Data Portability: Request your data in a portable format

To exercise these rights, contact us using the information below.

8. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. When you delete your account:

  • Personal data is deleted within 30 days
  • Aggregated, anonymized data may be retained for analytics
  • Backup copies are deleted according to our backup retention schedule

9. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

SuperParent is designed exclusively for parents and does not target or knowingly collect information from children under 13. Parents are solely responsible for supervising their children's use of the content provided through the app.

If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13 without parental consent, we will take steps to delete that information immediately.

10. International Users

If you are using SuperParent from outside the United States, please note that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States where our servers are located. By using the app, you consent to this transfer.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending you a notification for significant changes

Your continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Third-Party Links

Our app may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

13. California Privacy Rights

California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information is collected and to request deletion. Contact us to exercise these rights.
